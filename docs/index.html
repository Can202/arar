Arar


home - me - Older Version

Arar is a game for two players, the winner is that manage to insert more rings.

It's the red vs the blue , The Winner is the first to reach 5 points, or 15 points, or the one with the highest score in one minute, or 5 minutes. It is a very simple Game, to play with a friend.
